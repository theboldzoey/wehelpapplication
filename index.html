Introducing Hepta Protocol

Building a toolkit for all ESG endeavors Hepta Protocol is a one-stop ESG aggregator that optimizes resource allocation and engagement of all stakeholders in charitable work.

Context

With the rising focus of corporate ESG endeavors, We are approaching an era that actually cares about humanity in business.

Problem

But there is a missing piece… The infrastructure to properly manage and distribute all the resources. Corporates wasted resource and energy on initiatives not align with their business. Causes are not really supported, causing even more trouble. The effort are not easily traceable, hence difficult to accurately present.

Here's our solution

Corporate ESG made simple and effective.
Deploy money, see impact, grow their brand.

After 7 years of non-profit/ ESG work experiences and 100+ stakeholder interviews, Our team is now working on a tool that

  • Maximize the efficiency for enterprise ESG endeavors
  • Incentivize employers/ employees for social good causes
  • Ensure beneficiaries receive the resource they need

We believe this can unlock the virtuous cycle of upward mobility and impactful behaviors.
